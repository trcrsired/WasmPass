WasmPass Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 2, 2025
Last Updated: December 2, 2025

1. Introduction

WasmPass is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that demonstrates password, username, and PIN generation using WebAssembly. This privacy policy explains how WasmPass handles your information when you use the app.

2. Local Execution

WasmPass runs purely on your device. All password, username, and PIN generation happens locally in your browser using WebAssembly. There is no server component and no data is transmitted to us.

3. Information We Collect

  • No Personal Data Collection: WasmPass does not collect, store, or transmit personal information.
  • Generated Data: Any passwords, usernames, or PINs you create remain on your device.
  • Local Storage: If you choose to save generated data, it is stored only in your browser’s local storage.

4. Hosting

The WasmPass webpage is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may collect standard web server logs (e.g., IP address, browser type) for security and performance purposes. These logs are managed by Cloudflare and not by WasmPass.

5. Security

All generation occurs locally in your browser. No network requests are made to transmit generated data. You are responsible for managing and securing any data you choose to save.

6. Children’s Privacy

WasmPass is intended for general use and does not knowingly collect information from children.
